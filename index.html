Final: Smile for the Camera

My project is still a work in progress since I changed my topic a bit, but it's called "Smile for the Camera". I like to think of it as both a small photo album documentary of my life through various random photos, as well as my rather rocky relationship with photos and cameras and how I, being someone who hates being photographed or recorded, ironically fell in love with being on the other side of the camera. The journey of the story talks about the different types of pictures my family have taken of me and treasure, from baby photos, to pictures with fake smiles, to performance photos, to candid shots. As I explain the types of photos as well as the specific examples I use, I wanted the user to get a better understanding of my relationship with cameras.
